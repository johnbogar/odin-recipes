Asian Cajun Ribeye

This is a steak marinated in Asian and Cajun sauces. You only need 5 ingredients and it requires very little work!

    Ingredients

  • 1 lb Boneless Ribeye
  • Montreal Steak Seasoning
  • 1 Shot Glass of Tiger Sauce
  • 1 Shot Glass of Pickapeppa Sauce
  • 1 Shot Glass of Soy Sauce

      • Steps

  • Rub your steak with Montreal steak seasoning
  • Put tiger, pickapeppa, and soy sauces in a ziploc and add your steak
  • Let the steak sit in the marinade for at least 30 minutes, flipping it halfway through
  • Heat grill to 600-700 degrees fahrenheit then turn off the middle burner
  • Cook steak for 6 minutes on each side for medium rare
  • Enjoy!
