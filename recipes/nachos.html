Sausage Nachos

These nachos make a great dinner if you're short on time but don't want to sacrifice flavor!

    Ingredients

  • 1 Bag of Tortilla Chips
  • 1 Bag of Mexican Cheese Mix
  • 1 Carton of Pico de Gallo
  • Pickled Jalapeños
  • 1 lb of Jimmy Dean's Breakfast Sausage

      • Steps

  • Place chips on a large baking sheet
  • Add cheese, and bake on 350 degrees fahrenheit for 5 minutes
  • While the chips are in the oven, cook your sausage on medium heat
  • Pull chips from oven and add pico de gallo, jalapeños and sausage and enjoy!
